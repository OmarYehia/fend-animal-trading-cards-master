Domestic Cats

“ A Cat has the power to sometimes heal them selves by purring !! ”

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 46 cm (without tail)
  • Average lifespan: 9 years
  • Habitat: Human Habitats

The Cat is a small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and often referred to as the domestic cat; to distinguish it from wild members of the family. A house cat is valued by humans for companionship and for its ability to hunt rodents.
